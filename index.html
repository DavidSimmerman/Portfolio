I am a passionate software developer who has been programming since I was 11 or 12 years old in 2012. At the time, I was very into the video game Minecraft, and my friends and I wanted to make a server. We needed someone to program the plugins, so I got to work learning Java. As someone who loves problem solving and puzzels, I quickly fell in love with the concept of coding.

Over the next few years, I started to learn new programming languages on my own through YouTube, Udemy, and any other resources I could find. Unfortunately, I struggled to stay consistent with coding since I was busy with school and other extracurricular activities. I also gained a passion for computer hardware as well. I have built a couple of computers and constantly stay up to date watching news about the latest tech. I enjoy tearing things apart to see how they work and learning about how complex devices function.

I am a full stack developer with knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Python, and C++. I also have experience working with the Django Framework as well as Unreal Engine 4. I also have several IT skills such as building and repairing computers. I am constantly working to expand my knowledge on programming and computers.

I started a Computer Science Degree at Michigan State University, but later dropped out due to medial concerns. I then studied at The Tech Academy where I learned many of the skills I know today. On top of that, I took several Udemy courses primarily on C++ and game development. I am constantly working to learn more and improve my skills.
