- About -

I am a passionate software developer who has been programming since I was 11 or 12 years old in 2012. At the time, I was very into the video game Minecraft, and my friends and I wanted to make a server. We needed someone to program the plugins, so I got to work learning Java. As someone who loves problem solving and puzzels, I quickly fell in love with the concept of coding.

Over the next few years, I started to learn new programming languages on my own through YouTube, Udemy, and any other resources I could find. I worked with HTML, CSS, Java, C++, and Python. Unfortunately, I struggled to stay consistent with coding since I was busy with school and other extracurricular activities.

I am currently enrolled in The Tech Academy where I am learning to be a Software Developer. I am constantly working to learn more and improve my skills.

I would love to help work on any project with you! If you have any questions, contact me below!
